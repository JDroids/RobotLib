curvatureDrive

fun curvatureDrive(xSpeed: Double, zRotation: Double, isQuickTurn: Boolean): Unit

Similar to arcade drive, but instead of turning at a certain velocity the robot travels at a certain curvature.

Parameters

xSpeed - the forwards velocity to travel at

zRotation - the curvature at which to travel at

isQuickTurn - if this is set to true, the robot no longer travels in a curve and instead\ turns in place
